#### Solution
According to the frequency of letters appears normally in the text we can make assumption as follows:--
1. E with 13 %, X - E, k = 19.
2. T with  9 %, X - T, k =  4.
3. A with  8 %, X - A, k = 23.
4. O with  8 %, X - O, k =  9.
5. I with  8 %, X - I, k = 15.
6. N with  7 %, X - N, k = 10.
7. S with  7 %, X - S, k =  5.
8. H with  6 %, X - H, k = 16.
9. R with  6 %, X - R, k =  6.
